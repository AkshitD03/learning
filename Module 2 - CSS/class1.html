India

India is a land of immense diversity and rich cultural heritage. It is the seventh-largest country by area and the second-most populous country in the world. India shares its borders with several countries, including Pakistan, China, Nepal, Bhutan, Bangladesh, and Myanmar. The country is known for its secular and democratic values, allowing people to practice any religion freely. India has a vibrant history, with significant contributions from leaders like Mahatma Gandhi, who is revered for his non-violent struggle for independence.
